Setting up Videos for Sleepover

Basically, in order to load any of the above in game, There are two ways of doing this. Either, through The Roblox Player or Bloxstrap.

So, download any of the files you wish to be displayed for you in game, then, depending on the client type, move the files accordingly.

The Roblox Player

Open the path to your roblox clients folder, typically located under %LocalAppData%\Roblox\Versions, the client itself should be in the folder thats offset after version-2, if not, one of them should be it, just make sure its not roblox studio.

After, go into the folder called content, If you have a folder named TvVideos, then just drag the video/s in there and youre ready to join the game!

If you don't,

then, create the folder with the same name(Case sensitivity matters) and drag the video/s inside of it, and nooow youre done!

Bloxstrap

Open your Blostrap Menu, then click on the Mods, then, click on the button that says Open Mods Folder.
Inside the folder,if there isint one, create a folder called content, otherwise, go in it.
If you have a folder named TvVideos, then just drag the video/s in there and youre ready to join the game!

If you don't,

then, create the folder with the same name(Case sensitivity matters) and drag the video/s inside of it, and nooow youre done!

Hope it helps :>
